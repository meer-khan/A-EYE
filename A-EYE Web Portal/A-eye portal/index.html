An EYE for leading the Blind or Visually impaired people

A-EYE is a free app that helps the Blind and visually impaired people to live their life the same as that of a sighted individual. It uses artificial intelligence to enable a visually impaired to recognize different objects, products, colors, currency notes, scenes, facial expressions, and read documents and short texts.

Our Mission

A-EYE's mission is to help the 285 million people suffering with visual impairments by providing a convenient, affordable, yet innovative technology that gives them a higher level of independence and make them more self-sufficient in their daily lives.

An EYE for leading the blind

A-EYE is a free mobile camera application that helps the Blind and visually impaired people to live their life the same as that of a sighted individual. It uses artificial intelligence and computer vision to enable a visually impaired to recognize different objects, products, colors, currency notes, scenes, facial expressions, and read documents and short texts. A-EYE has a guide panel where the Guide of blind or visually impaired people can keep track of his activities.The app is an effort to provide assistance to visually impaired people so that they may become more self-sufficient. A-EYE is completely based on voice control mechanism.Just speak up your desire task and let A-EYE to make it easy for you.
